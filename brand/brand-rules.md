# ONES Brand Rules

## Logos
- Preferred logo: horizontal standard ONES logo.
- Allowed variants: light and dark assets only.
- Prohibited uses: recolor, distort, stretch, rotate, apply gradient, shadow, glow, blur, outline, or any visual effects.
- Do not separate icon and wordmark into custom compositions.
- Use provided logo assets only.

## Product Logos
- Product logo format must be: icon + ONES + product name.
- Supported product names include Project, Wiki, and TestCase.
- Do not invent new lockups, spacing systems, or icon-wordmark arrangements.

## Visual Style
- Keep interfaces clean, minimal, modern, and tech-oriented.
- Use generous whitespace and neutral backgrounds.
- Avoid visual clutter and decorative styling that weakens information hierarchy.

## Colors
- Use design tokens only.
- Do not hardcode random brand colors in components.
- Gradients are allowed for page or section backgrounds only.
- Never apply gradients to logos.
